Magic Blast: Mystery Puzzle

Unleash the Magic, Solve the Mystery, and Unlock Hidden Secrets!

About the Game

Mystery Puzzle invites players on an exciting journey to uncover ancient secrets and solve challenging puzzles. As you explore a world full of enchantment, you’ll blast through magical puzzles, use powerful boosters, and unravel the mysteries of a long-lost magical realm. Solve hundreds of increasingly difficult levels, unlock hidden relics, and face the challenges of magical forces as you progress in this immersive, puzzle adventure.

Features

  • Mystical Puzzle Gameplay: Solve challenging and unique puzzles filled with magical symbols and enchanted objects.

  • Powerful Boosters: Use magic-filled boosters to help you overcome tricky puzzles and clear large sections of the board.

  • Mysterious Storyline: Explore a captivating story about ancient magic, hidden treasures, and a long-forgotten magical world.

  • Enchanting Visuals: Beautiful, magical environments filled with glowing symbols, mystical lights, and otherworldly creatures.

  • Regular Updates: New levels and features added frequently to keep the magic alive.

  • Offline Play: Fully playable offline, so you can solve puzzles anytime, anywhere.

How to Play

  • Match and Blast:

    Tap on groups of 3 or more matching magical items to clear them from the board and score points.

  • Use Magical Boosters:

    Combine special items to create powerful boosts and clear larger sections of the board.

  • Complete Puzzles:

    Solve levels by completing objectives, such as clearing certain items or reaching a target score.

  • Unlock Secrets:

    As you progress, uncover hidden relics and mysterious elements that propel you deeper into the magical world.

  • Explore the Story:

    Solve puzzles to unlock chapters of the mystery and uncover the secrets of the ancient magical realm.

Characters

  • Liora the Sorceress

    Liora is a wise and powerful sorceress, gifted with the ability to see glimpses of the future. Her magical prowess helps guide you through the more difficult puzzles. Her mysterious past and cryptic prophecies are essential for unlocking the secrets of the world.

  • Aether the Wizard

    Aether is a mischievous but skilled wizard who specializes in unlocking ancient artifacts. His charm and intelligence make him a valuable companion as you solve puzzles and discover hidden relics.

  • Nyx the Spirit Guide

    Nyx is a mysterious spirit who guides players through the game with her ethereal presence. She communicates through magical whispers, offering helpful hints when you’re stuck on particularly tough puzzles.

  • Grimble the Keeper

    Grimble is the keeper of ancient knowledge, tasked with guarding the secrets of the magical realm. His old-world wisdom and eccentric personality make him an intriguing character who provides valuable clues to help players unlock deeper mysteries.
